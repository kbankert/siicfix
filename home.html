Meet Kate

SIIC Master, Kate Bankert

Early in my career, I started experiencing dibilitating digestive distress. My doctors took tests and prescribed remedies, but my results returned normal and my symptoms remained. Determined to find a path back to feeling "normal," I put on the white coat and created my own protocol based on what I found worked for me. It took me ten years to peel back the onion, but I finally cracked the case and learned how Stress, Inflammation, Immune deficiency and Candida overgrowth were making me sick. I created The SIIC Fix to pass along the shortcuts I learned along the way.

At the heart of my methodology is Moduration. You're the expert your health for the long haul. My role is help you find a simple path toward sustainable change and to ensure that you to never feel alone, stuck or overwhelmed along the way. My approach to change incorporates best practices across psychology, economics, neuroscience, and agile development. I am a member of the American College of Nutrition and have degrees from from Cornell University and Stanford University.

Read more about my path from SIIC Chic to No GAS Girl at www.nogasplan.com.
