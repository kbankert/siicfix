HealthHacks

Sharpen your creativity

brain

THE SCIENCE: Creativity requires both the left and the right brain, but oftentimes the left brain will dominate when we try to problem-solve. When we are fully immersed in a physical activity, we quite the left brain and give the right brain a voice at the table.

THE HACK: The next time you are in search of a good idea, take ten minutes to draw the object in front of you. Be sure to let go of perfection and fully immerse yourself to make room for the creative aha!

Berry your cravings

Stress - Gut

THE SCIENCE: Oftentimes our sugar cravings are not our cravings, rather they are from our stress hormones wanting to activate a response or from the fungus and bacteria housed in our gut wanting to multiply - both of which can be detrimental to our gut health.

THE HACK: The next time you have a craving for sugary food, regain control by simply ask yourself who is having the craving. If the craving is too strong to ignore, give in to it with a handful of fresh berries.

Roll your burrito

food

THE SCIENCE: Seaweed is rich in vitamins and minerals, including iodine, selenium, calcium and iron. Additionally, it is excellent for gut health because certain properties can strengthen the intestinal lining, aid in digestion and increase the good bacteria in the gut.

THE HACK: The next time you make a breakfast sandwich or burrito at home, replace your usual bread or tortilla with a seaweed wrap. Be sure to wait a few minutes to let the food cool to keep the wrap intact.

About Me

Early in my career, I started experiencing dibilitating digestive discomfort. My doctors took tests and prescribed remedies, but my results returned normal and my symptoms remained. Determined to find a path back to feeling "normal," I put on the white coat and created my own protocol based on what I found worked for me. It took me ten years to peel back onion, but I finally cracked the case and today I am my happiest, healthiest, vibrant self. Read more about my path at www.nogasplan.com.

My approach is to help you to find your path back to "normal" by guiding you through a simple process of making sustainable lifestyle changes that will relieve your symptoms for the long haul. My goal is for you to never feel alone, stuck or overwhelmed along the way so that you can get back to feeling great, faster.
